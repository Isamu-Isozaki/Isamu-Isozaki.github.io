What is CAS?

It is a sort of an Extra curricular portion of the IB program. The CAS project is a work of collaboration to do an extra curricular activity. Hence, here, I thought it will be a great opportunity to collaborate with my friends to tackle some intriguing questions that I may not be able to solve on my own. So, basically, this website attempts to answer large questions which I may not be able to do on my self.

Who will be participating in this CAS activity?

My two wonderful friends who will be answering big questions with me are Ryota Okamoto and Kei Okada.
